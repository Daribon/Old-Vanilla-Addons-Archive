GoodInspect version 1.0.2 by usea - 2005
an inspect function improvement addon for World of Warcraft

increases inspect range and allows inspection of opposing faction members
adds guild info to inspect window a la your character sheet
also adds a key binding for inspection
you'll find the "Inspect Target" entry in the key bindings under "Good Inspect"


changes in 1.0.2
- you can now inspect members of the opposing faction
